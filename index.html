BAROS

Verifiable trust for local work.

A neighborhood marketplace where reputation is real and payments are guaranteed — without anyone needing to understand blockchain.

Social vouching — Neighbors vouch for each other. Each vouch is a compressed NFT on Solana, creating portable, tamper‑proof reputation.
Privacy‑preserving identity — Verify you're a real person with zero‑knowledge proofs. No ID data is ever exposed to the platform.
Smart contract escrow — Payment is held in a Solana program. Funds release only when the client confirms the job is done. Instant settlement.
Invisible blockchain — Users pay with mobile money or card. Under the hood, everything settles in seconds on Solana for less than a cent.
Everyone is both client and provider — Post a job, offer a service, apply for work, or hire someone. One account does it all.

BAROS is a Solana hackathon project. It demonstrates how local trust can be rebuilt with on‑chain reputation and escrow — no crypto UX required.

Three layers of trust

1

Social vouching

Neighbors, past clients, friends vouch for you. Real humans. No bots. Every vouch is an on‑chain compressed NFT — portable, verifiable reputation.

2

Identity, not anonymity

Optional gov ID verification via zero‑knowledge proofs. You prove you're real without exposing your data. No ghosts.

3

Escrow + QR release

Money sits in a Solana smart contract. Client scans QR → funds release in seconds. Provider gets paid. No more “I'll transfer later.”

Blockchain is invisible. Users never see a wallet or seed phrase.

Everyone plays both sides

You can offer a service, request a service, post a job, or apply for one — all from the same account.

I need a service client

1
Post a job — describe, set price, location. Nearby providers see it.
2
Pick a provider — see trust score, vouches, verifiable work history.
3
Fund escrow — pay via mobile money or card. Money locked in smart contract.
4
Release & vouch — scan QR after job done. Funds release instantly. Leave a vouch (on-chain).

I offer a service provider

1
Create service listing — e.g., “Generator repair — Lekki — ₦15k”. Always discoverable.
2
Receive booking requests — clients find you via map/search, request a time.
3
Accept & complete — do the work. Client releases payment via QR.
4
Build reputation — each job adds to your on-chain trust score. Portable proof.

Apply for a posted job

Browse open jobs in your neighborhood → apply with one tap. If selected, the escrow flow starts automatically.
